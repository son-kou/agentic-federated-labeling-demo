## Research Question

for prostate MRI that supports federated learning, audit transparency, and
active-learning-guided human QC — without sharing raw imaging data?

## Privacy and Audit Statement

- Raw MRI volumes remained on their originating site at all times.
- Only the following were transmitted to the FL coordinator:
  - Model weight delta norms (scalar values, no weights).
  - Aggregated QC statistics (pass rate, review rate, mean Dice).
  - Case counts (processed / needing review).
- Audit JSON files are written to `outputs/site_reports/<site>_audit.json`
  and `outputs/fl_rounds/` for full traceability.
- In production, differential privacy (Gaussian mechanism) should be applied
  to gradient updates before transmission.
